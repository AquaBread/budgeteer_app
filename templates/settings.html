Getting Started

1. Set your income

Start by entering your annual salary above and saving. The app uses this to estimate your monthly income and projected savings.

2. Configure your accounts

Go to Accounts in the top navigation and add at least one debit account (and optionally credit accounts) that you spend from.

3. Create your monthly budget

Open the Budgets page and choose a month in YYYY-MM format. For each category, enter how much you plan to spend that month, then save.

4. Add transactions

On the Transactions page, record your spending manually: pick an account, date, amount, and category. Use “Expense (−)” for spending and “Refund/Income (+)” when money comes back in.

5. Set up recurring expenses

Use the Recurring page for fixed monthly costs (rent, subscriptions, etc.). Add each recurring expense once, then on the Transactions page you can apply this month’s recurring expenses in a single click.

6. Read the dashboard

The Dashboard shows:

  • Your estimated monthly income.
  • Total budgeted vs spent so far this month.
  • Whether you’re ahead or behind your plan (pro-rata variance).
  • A suggested daily cap for the rest of the month to stay on track.
  • Projected savings for the current month.

7. Adjust as you go

As the month progresses, tweak your budgets, add or edit transactions, and watch how your remaining allowance and projected savings change.

8. Organize your categories

Use the Category Groups page to group categories (e.g., Housing, Food, Transport) so your budgets and charts are easier to read.
